About

Don’t just dream it, drive it!
An overnight event for aspiring entrepreneurs, where latent talents are discovered and their future moulded. Here is a risk free environment to evaluate your B-Plans. Solve real time problems and come up with solutions overnight and find out if you have what it takes to leverage innovative thinking and make it big.
An educative event where the teams will be taught the basics of ideation, innovation, B-plans and marketing. Teams will discover real time problems the world is facing and will have to come up with a probable solution to it by conceptualizing a prototype, all overnight!

Number of participants per team : 3 to 5 .

Prize : Worth INR 60,000.

Register Now

Stage 1:

The complete event will be an overnight process where students need to conceptualize a prototype and develop a Business Plan.
The entire process of Business development will be carried out through a series of modules on practr.in
The different phases will include:

  • Idea projections
  • Market opportunity
  • Size up the competition
  • Prototype conceptualization
  • Cash flow statements

Stage 2:

Post the development process, teams will be submitting a B-Plan which shall be evaluated by a panel of judges. Ten teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

Stage 3:

This will be the final round of Business Marathon.
The shortlisted teams will have to pitch a complete a Business plan to the panel of judges.

Rules:

  1. Complete work for the event should be started from scratch at the venue
  2. Participants are supposed to bring 3 laptops along with chargers.
  3. A total of 6 members can be there in a team. A student can be a part of only one team.
  4. Students pursuing under graduation can only participate in BM.
  5. One team is allowed to submit to give only business plan.

Note: The final round judgment will be solely done by the mentors and panel of judges.
