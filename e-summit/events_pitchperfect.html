About

It’s said, “For every minute that passes, a billionaire might lose a cent of their money or earn it.” In this world of crushing competition, time is money and money is time.
Pitch Perfect provides a platform for early stage startups to pitch their business idea in front of a panel of potential investors and to perceive their idea through the investors’ perspective. Acting as a profound stage for upcoming startups to broadcast their ideas, Pitch Perfect exposes them to a panel who might fund them up to 1 million within 10 minutes of their Business Pitch.
Whether or not a financial transaction occurs, the main objective behind this platform is to provide exposure to potential entrepreneurs and experts.

Click here to register

On successful registration the teams will receive a confirmation mail.

Round 1:

Interested participants will be given an online questionnaire by F6S that is to be answered. Based on the answers, top 50 teams will be selected for the next round.

Round 2:

The shortlisted teams will have to submit a video of their elevator pitch
Top 10 teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

Round 3:

Teams shortlisted for this round will have to pay a registration fee of Rs. 500 per team.
The final round will be held at the campus of RVCE.
The 10 participating teams have to give a Business Pitch in front a panel of Investors who will be judging the pitch.

Rules:

  1. The startups participating in the event should at least be a year old irrespective of any profit being made or not.
  2. Each team is allowed to submit only one Elevator Pitch in Round 1.
  3. The teams will be funded on the basis of their potential and also the investors’ interest which will be a sum of up to 10 lakhs (singularly/collaboratively).

Note:

  1. The investment by the investors is subject to the investors’ interest in the idea being pitched.
  2. The Entrepreneurship Cell does not guarantee investment from the panel.
