About

To initiate and run a great business one needs to have an idea that has a great business plan. In order to provide the right platform and support for students with great ideas, we present to you our national level B-Plan competition. The event allows students to pitch their ideas in front of mentors and investors, also receive exposure in the credibility of their Business Plans and the market feasibility of their plans.
Embark upon a plan, win cash and startup!

Number of participants per team : 2 to 5 .

Prize : Worth INR 40,000.

Register Now

On successful registration the teams will receive a confirmation mail.

Round 1:

The registered teams will have to submit a Business Plan, the format of which will be specified in the confirmation mail.
Teams will be selected in this round on the basis of clarity of the B-Plan submitted, and then will be called up for the final round. A total of 8 teams will be shortlisted for the final round, and the list of the same will be released three days prior to the event.
The teams have to submit their B-Plan within 5 days from the date of registration.

Round 2:

The second round will be an offline round which will happen in the RVCE campus.
Shortlisted teams need to give a business pitch in front of a panel of judges.

Rules:

  1. A total of 4 members can be there in a team and at least 3 members should be present for the final round.
  2. B-Plans submitted after 5 days of registration is mandatory otherwise will be rejected.
  3. A student can be a part of only one team.
  4. Only students pursuing under-graduation can participate in Expediens.
  5. One team is allowed to submit only one business plan.

Note:

  1. Shortlisting of teams for the final round will be done by a panel of experts along with the organizing team of E-Summit’17.
  2. The final round will be solely judged by the panel of experts present.
  3. Extension of deadlines is not permissible in any case. Teams which do not follow the rules will be disqualified from the event.
